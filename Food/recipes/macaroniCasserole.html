Macaroni casserole

Ingredients

  • 6dl Macaroni of your choice
  • 1 big onion
  • 400-600g mince meat
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoonsground curry
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 3 eggs
  • 7dl milk

Method

  1. Fry the mince and onion together in a pan.
  2. Once the meat is halfway done, throw in all your spices and mix well.
  3. Cook the macaronic according to the macaroni package instructions.
  4. Once the macaroni and mince are cooked, mix them together in a medium to large sized baking dish.
  5. Take a bowl and mix the eggs and milk together, whisk lightly until the eggs fully break apart.
  6. Pour the milk and egg mixture into your baking dish containing the macaroni and mince.
  7. Heat your oven to 200C and cook your dish for around 1h or until done (usually takes 45min).
