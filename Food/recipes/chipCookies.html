Cookies

Great homemade cookies that crumble and melt in your mouth!

Ingredients

  • 350g flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 250g butter
  • 300g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 bag of smarties, M&M's or chocolate chip

Method

  1. Sift the flour, baking soda, baking powder in to a bowl. Set a side for later.
  2. Blend together the butter and sugar until. Beat and or mix until pale and fluffy, then beat in the egg and vanilla.
  3. Gradually beat in the sifted dry ingredients to form a stiff dough.
  4. Roll the dough into small balls. Think of a squash ball or smaller than a golf ball. These will expand greatly in the oven. Then place on ungreased baking trays, spaced well between each other.
  5. Press several Smarties/M&M's/Chocolate chips into each ball, flattening the ball slightly.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes and/or until pale golden brown.
  7. Leave on trays 5 minutes and remove to cooling racks to cool completely. This allows the cookies to stiften up slightly.
