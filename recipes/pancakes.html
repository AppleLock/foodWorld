Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2dl all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar
  • (optional) 1/2 teaspoon cardamom
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2dl milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Small butter piece for pan

Method

  1. Combine all dry ingredients and stir them together.
  2. Beat the eggs.
  3. Combine milk and the beaten eggs with dry ingredients.
  4. Whisk it all together until the ingredients have combined well.
  5. Wait 5min for the batter to settle.
  6. Set pan to medium heat and melt the butter in to it.
  7. Once the butter has melted and smeared itself in to the pan, you can remove any excess butter with a paper towel so it doesn't burn.
  8. Pour small amounts of pancake batter on to the pan. Wait for the bubbles to appear on the top of the pancake.
  9. Once bubbles have formed on the top, start by gently removing it and flipping it over. If bubbles aren't appearing relatively quick on your pancake, your pan isn't hot enough.
  10. Repeat step 8-9 until you have finished your batter.
