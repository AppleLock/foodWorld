Artichoke pasta

Ingredients

  • 4 artichokes
  • 1 onion
  • Olive oil
  • Kale
  • Cooking cream (we're using Planti cooking cream)
  • Pasta (around 200g)
  • Fond 'du chef' vegetable (kasvisfondi)

Method

  1. Warm oven to 200 degrees.
  2. Chop 2 of your artichokes in to circular chip shapes. Chop the rest of your artichoke and onion in to small cubes.
  3. Mix around your artichoke chips in salt and oil
  4. Place your chips on an tray and spread across evenly. Bake at 200 degrees until crisp and golden brown.
  5. Remove the stem from your kale and keep the leafy greens. Chop them in to small bits as well.
  6. Fry your onion and artichoke until soft.
  7. Add your cream and 2 table spoons of Fond 'du chef'.
  8. Add 2/3 of your kale in to the frying pan.
  9. Cook your pasta as per instruciton.
  10. Once the pasta completes drain it and add it to the pan.
  11. Make sure the pasta is evenly coated and it's ready to serve. Use the rest of your kale as topping on the pasta.
