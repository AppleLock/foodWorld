Blini (Finnish)

Ingredients

  • 4 dl milk
  • 20g yeast
  • 2 dl buckwheat flour
  • 1 dl white flour
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 egg whites
  • 200 g sour cream (Valio kermaviili)

Method

  1. Start the blini making process by mixing the yeast with luke warm milk. Mix well until the yeast is fully dissolved.
  2. In to the mixture pour in your buckwheat and white flour. Once they have melded together cover with a kitchen towel and let sit minimum 1h at room temperature.
  3. Add the egg yolks, sour cream and a dash of salt to the mixture. Beat separately egg whites with a electric beater preferably until they are fluffy and stick to the beater.Mix all the ingriedients together.
  4. Cook your blinis with butter / oil on a pan. Set them aside under a plate and cover with aluminum foil to prevent them from cooling. Letting them sit on a plate open before serving is fine too.
  5. Feel free to serve with smetana, salmon caviar, and other treats.
