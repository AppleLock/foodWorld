Sweet potato soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoons medium curry paste
  • 500g sweet potato
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 dl red lentils
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 L chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 dl coconut milk or cream

Method

  1. Dice all your vegetables.
  2. Put your 1 L of broth to a boil then to a simmer
  3. Heat the oil in a large frying or wok pan.
  4. On medium heat, fry your vegetables with the curry paste for around 5-10 minutes. Just to sweat them a bit and get a nice coating.
  5. Put all your vegetables from the pan to the cooking pot with the broth. Boil them until soft.
  6. Once your vegetables are done take the soup off the heat. Add your coconut milk or cream. Then blend the soup with an electric hand blender or you can do it in a regular blender.
