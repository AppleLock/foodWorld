Olivier salad

Ingredients

  • 3-4 medium potatoes
  • 3 large carrots
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 can of green peas ( about 125 gr)
  • 6-8 pickled gherkins or pickles
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2dl of mayonnaise
  • Pork sausage (preference only, usually use without!)

Method

  1. Boil your potatoes and carrots for about 20-25 min. You donâ€™t want to overcook your vegetables, because we want them nice and crisp.
  2. Boil your eggs for 10 min in a separate pan.
  3. Afterwards let you eggs and vegetables cool down to room temperature.
  4. Cut your gherkins, carrots, potatoes and boiled eggs into cubes.
  5. Mix all the ingredients together with mayonnaise, salt and pepper
