Tomato soup

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ celeryn
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 can of peeled tomatos
  • 1.5L vegetable stock
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 1 onion
  • Table spoon tomato paste
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Boil the stock in a separate pot.
  2. Mince the carrots, celery and onion and fry them up in in some olive oil.
  3. Once golden brown thrown in the tomato paste and coat evenly.
  4. Throw in the tomato’s and fry them with the rest of the ingredients.
  5. After a while throw all the ingredients in to the vegetable stock (or the other way around). Bring to a boil then down to a simmer.
  6. Serve with some grilled cheese or garlic bread for extra cozyness.
