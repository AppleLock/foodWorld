Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1/2 dl dry breadcrumbs
  • 1 dl milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 dl grated parmesan
  • 1/4 dl finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 800g of mince
  • 1 chopped onion

Method

  1. Soak the bread crumbs in the milk, stir them so it forms a smooth white paste.
  2. Mix the rest of the ingredients together, except the eggs and the breadcrumb mixture.
  3. Beat the eggs and blend them in to the mixture.
  4. Now add the bread crumb paste and blend all the ingredients well.
  5. Form medium sized ball shapes in the palm of your hand and place them on a baking sheet.
  6. Heat the oven to 180C and cook the meatballs for about 40min.
