Broccoli casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 large broccoli
  • 200g of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 dl quinoa
  • 60g spinach
  • Bunch of spring onion
  • 1L of vegetable stock

Method

  1. Place your quinoa in a baking dish
  2. Boil up your 1L of vegetable stock
  3. Chop your spring onions and place them with spinach on top of the quinoa in the baking dish.
  4. Pour cheese on top of the quinoa and stock mixture so it evenly covers the bakind dish.
  5. Place the baking dish at 175C for around 20-35 min or until the liquid has cooked the quinoa or until the cheese has gotten a golden brown crust formed.
  6. While baking, cook your broccoli. Once the quinoa has cooked take your broccoli and decorate your dish with it.
