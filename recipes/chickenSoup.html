Chicken soup

Ingredients

  • 2 table spoons of oil
  • 600 grams of chopped chicken filets
  • Dash of salt
  • Chili seasoning
  • 1-2 garlic cloves (optional)
  • 1/2 or 3/4 of a leek finely chopped
  • 4-6 big carrots
  • 1 red or yellow paprika
  • 1,6-2 litres of water depending, if you are doing two soups - 1,3 each if you are
  • 2.5 dl of whole grain or white rice
  • 2 vegetable soup cubes
  • 200 grams of sulatejuusto
  • 100 grams of tomato pyree
  • 200 grams of peas
  • 1 pot of coriander or parsley

Method

  1. Take the chicken out to room temperature approximately half an hour beforehand cooking it.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan or pot and fry the chicken. Season with salt and chili seasoning.
  3. Chop the garlic and leek. Wash the leek leaves well and chop finely.
  4. Chop carrots into circles and chop also the paprika into cubes.
  5. Add the chopped ingredients among the chicken and fry all for a bit.
  6. Add water, rice and soup cubes. Let the concoction boil at a medium heat until the rice is cooked. Stir once in a while.
  7. Once rice is cooked, add the cheese (sulatejuusto), tomato pyree and peas. It's important to chop the cheese and stir in order for it to melt properly.
  8. Taste, and season to your liking.
  9. Chop and sprinkle fresh coriander or parsley on the soup.
