Pasta carbonara

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of bacon or 250g pancetta
  • couple of garlic cloves
  • pasta for two
  • Parmesan or Pecorino Romano
  • 4 eggs yolks

Method

  1. Cook your pancetta or bacon with the garlic. Use whole or lightly crushed cloves. We just want to release some of the flavoring in to the meat.
  2. Grate your cheese and mix them well with the egg yolks
  3. Boil your pasta. Remove the garlic from the pan with the meat. Once the pasta is done throw it in the bacon mixture and give it a very light fry. Mix your cheese and egg mixture in to the same pan. Be careful that the pan isn't too hot otherwise your egg mixture will turn in to a scramble and it will ruin your dish.
