Banana Loaf

Ingredients

  • 150g butter, softned, plus a little extra for your bread tin
  • 150g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 3 bananas
  • 50g icing sugar
  • Optional: Banana chips

Method

  1. Butter the loaf tin so there's a light even layer.
  2. Blend together the flour and baking powder.
  3. Mash 3 bananas in a bowl.
  4. Whisk/Mix together 140g softned butter and 140g sugar. You need to do this until they are light and fluffy in texture.
  5. Once it's turned in to a light texture, add 2 beaten eggs with a little bit of your flour
  6. Blend together the remaining flour, little at a time.
  7. Add in the mashed banana in to the bowl and mix well.
  8. Line the tin loaf with baking paper and pour in your mixture.
  9. Heat the oven to 180C and bake for 30-40min
  10. A way to tell if the loaf is done is to stick it with a wooden skewer, if it comes out clean it's ready.
  11. Cool the loaf on a wire rack for 15-20 minutes.
  12. Mix 50g icing sugar with 2-3 tablespoons of water to make a runny icing.
  13. Drizzle the icing on top of your cooled loaf and decorate with a handful of banana chips if you wish.
