Drunken potato

Ingredients

  • 6 potatos
  • Salt
  • Oil (preferably olive or truffle oil)

Method

  1. Don't peel but wash your potatos extremely well.
  2. Cut them lenghtwise to get two halves of a potato.
  3. Put the potato in a bowl. Salt and oil them up heavily.
  4. Heat your oven to 200C. Place your potatos facing skin up on a baking plate and sheet. Cook for about 20min or until they get a golden brown skin.
